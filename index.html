Our Latest Projects

At DOC AI, we are creating innovative solutions that leverage AI and emerging technologies to transform healthcare. Our projects are designed to improve patient care, enhance mental health monitoring, and ensure data privacy in healthcare applications.

AI Agent Assistant for Lifestyle and Health

Our AI-powered assistant uses large language models (LLM) to interact with users via voice and text. It provides lifestyle guidance, preliminary diagnoses based on symptom descriptions, and personalized instructions to prevent complications. The assistant also tracks health behaviors and offers reminders for medication.

Mobile App for Proactive Mental Health Monitoring

This mobile app monitors user behaviors such as scrolling patterns, call tone, sleep quality, and screen time to detect potential mental health issues like depression and anxiety. It ensures privacy by not storing sensitive information, offering early signs and insights into the user's well-being.

Federated Learning Research in Healthcare

We are advancing healthcare AI through federated learning, enabling secure AI model training without sharing sensitive patient data. This ensures privacy while improving healthcare outcomes. Our research also explores human-computer interaction (HCI) to design user-friendly interfaces for better patient and provider experiences.

FAQ

+

How do I get started with DocAI?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us with your project idea or challenges, and we'll discuss how our AI and technology solutions can help you. From there, we'll design a customized approach to meet your needs.

+

Why are you researching federated learning in healthcare?

We believe healthcare innovation must prioritize privacy. Our research in federated learning aims to protect sensitive patient data while still enabling powerful AI-driven insights. This research is completely non-profit — we contribute it openly to help build a secure, ethical, and collaborative healthcare future through open-source technologies.

+

How does your research benefit the healthcare ecosystem?

By researching privacy-preserving AI methods like federated learning, we help ensure that innovation doesn’t come at the cost of personal data. Our work is openly shared to support researchers, developers, and healthcare organizations who want to build responsible, scalable, and user-focused solutions.

+

Do you only build solutions based on client requests?

No — we also proactively design and build our own solutions based on unmet needs in healthcare. From mental health tools to AI assistants, we create innovative applications even without external demand, guided by real-world problems and emerging technologies.

+

What else do you work on besides research?

We also develop healthcare-focused web and mobile applications, including intelligent assistants, mental health monitoring apps, and teleconsultation platforms — all built around user privacy and personalized care.

+

Is your research available to the public?

Yes! We are committed to open-source. Our research findings, frameworks, and datasets (when ethically shareable) are made available to the public to accelerate healthcare innovation and ethical AI development worldwide.

+

What makes your healthcare solutions unique?

We focus on personalized, data-driven solutions using the latest advancements in AI, machine learning, and federated learning. Our emphasis on privacy and patient-centric design ensures a secure and seamless user experience.
